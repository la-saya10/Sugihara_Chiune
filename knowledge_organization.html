Theoretical Model

The theoretical model maps the relations between the topic of this project — Chiune Sugihara — and ten entities identified in the related Wikipedia content. Each relation is expressed as a directed link from a subject to an object, capturing both biographical facts and the cultural memory that has formed around them.
