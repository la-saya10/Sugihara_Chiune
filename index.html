About this project

This LODLAM project focuses on Chiune Sugihara (杉原千畝, 1900–1986), a Japanese diplomat who, during World War II, issued thousands of transit visas to Jewish refugees in Kaunas, Lithuania. The project takes the English Wikipedia article on Sugihara as its source text and produces a structured, interlinked dataset using TEI/XML, RDF/Turtle, and Linked Open Data principles.

The goal is to demonstrate how a historical figure and the entities surrounding him can be described with formal knowledge organisation methods — from a theoretical model of conceptual relationships through to a machine-readable RDF dataset aligned with international authority files (Wikidata, VIAF, LCSH).

Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chiune_Sugihara
