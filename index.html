About this project

This LODSCH (Linked Open Data in Information Science and Cultural Heritage) project focuses on Sugihara Chiune (杉原千畝, 1900-1986), a Japanese diplomat who, during World War II, issued thousands of transit visas to Jewish refugees in Lithuania. The project takes the English Wikipedia article on him as its source text and produces a structured, interlinked dataset using TEI P5, RDF/OWL, and Linked Open Data principles.

The goal is to demonstrate how a cultural heritage entity can be described with formal knowledge organisation methods — from a mind map of conceptual relationships through to a machine-readable RDF dataset aligned with international authority files (Wikidata, VIAF, LCSH).

Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chiune_Sugihara
