Theoretical Model

The theoretical model maps the relations between the topic of this project — Chiune Sugihara — and entities identified in the related Wikipedia content. Each relation is expressed as a directed link from a subject to an object, capturing both biographical facts and the cultural memory that has formed around them.

Conceptual Model

The conceptual model formalizes the theoretical model using established vocabularies: FOAF for people and organizations, Schema.org for places and creative works, CIDOC CRM for historical events, SKOS for Righteous Among the Nations as a controlled concept, and Dublin Core Terms for bibliographic relations. Where no existing term fits precisely, the closest available property is used as a substitute.
